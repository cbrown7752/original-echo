About Us

Echo is an administration Discord bot that runs all your basic commands such as but not limited to kicking members, banning members, create polls for people to vote on, display gifs, pictures, videos and many more with additions always being added.


Echo's developers come from a wide range of individual's from all of the United States and even outside of the states. They all work very hard on the bot to ensure that it is running at its full potential if you ever run into a problem with the bot feel free to send us an email echobot.offical@gmail.com and someone will get back to you as soon as possible about your problem
